Dear Bleyn

A girl and her dog played full of whim.

But the girl got separated from the dog in devastated consequences.

An angel came, the girl prayed to reunite with the dog.

The girl traversed lands accompanied by the angel.

But when the girl found the dog, she didn't hug him. She simply walked away.

It was then that the angel realised that it was the dog's prayer, and not the girl's.

The dog, dying of age, breath at the sun downrise.

The angel wept with melancholy and returned from where it came.
