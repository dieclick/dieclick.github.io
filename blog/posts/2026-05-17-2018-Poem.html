Seems like i had an old poem in my email, resent to me back in 2018 (wow its been 8 years) Seems like it was planned for a line in a game, or something like that. It's something I didn't even remember it existed.

I will dance with you and death until vanishing from existence.

How long can we dance until the end is near?

I won't let your dead cold fingers go until we are done with our dance, my love.

Death is always near and always watches, and so I will always try to notice it because of you.

It is not our mistakes, it is our broken time that will let the dance be over.

So I will dance with all my heart just for you.

Please come back with me in my arms as I was before with you.

Learn quickly and keep moving swiftly, because I don't want our dance to end.

Even until you come, I will be waiting until I go.

And he leaves into a trail of dust and shine.

And she, the one left standing, whispered into the dissolving air

Yes, I will, my love.
